## ğŸ¯ Core Concept: Modular Communities

### What is an Urban Village?

An Urban Village is a **self-contained, tokenized community economy** built around a specific resource or industry vertical. Each village:

1. **Tokenizes a Resource** - Natural wine, local food, art, real estate, events, etc.
2. **Issues Fungible Tokens** - Community currency for rewards, payments, and governance
3. **Mints Provenance NFTs** - Blockchain-verified authenticity for physical goods
4. **Builds Community Treasury** - Transaction fees fund local development
5. **Enables Democratic Governance** - Token holders vote on allocation and initiatives

### Why "Modular"?

Each village is **independent but composable**:
- Wine village partners with food village for farm-to-table events
- Art village collaborates with real estate village for gallery spaces  
- Music village teams up with venue network for concerts
- **Network effects compound** as villages interconnect
